Ingredients

  • 75 kg Dimetrodon meat
  • 20 kg carrots
  • 20 kg green beans
  • 20 kg potatoes
  • 20 l diced tomatoes
  • 20 l dinosaur stock
  • 10 l balsamic vinegar
  • 10 l olive oil
  • 100 grams of spices: rosemary, parsley, oregano
  • 10 grams of salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop the Dimetrodon meat into cubes
  2. Chop the carrots into discs
  3. Chop the green beans into lengths a few centimetres long
  4. Chop the potatoes into bit-sized chunks
  5. Place all the ingredients into the slow cooker
  6. Stew on low for 6 hours
